# Batch Brief: Axion, Strong CP, and Peccei-Quinn Refinement

## Goal

Build a narrow Stage 21 refinement batch for the remaining Schwartz late-Standard-Model
tail around the strong-CP problem, Peccei-Quinn symmetry, axion language, and the
pseudo-Goldstone bridge that makes the axion branch intelligible.

This batch should stay tightly connected to the existing nonperturbative-QCD topology
branch and the existing Standard-Model symmetry-breaking branch, without drifting into a
generic beyond-the-Standard-Model catalog.

## Target Topic

- `axion`
- `strong CP`
- `Peccei-Quinn symmetry`

## Batch Purpose

This batch is meant to capture the smallest reusable neighborhood that makes Schwartz's
axion and strong-CP vocabulary queryable as an organized topic cluster rather than as a
pair of disconnected index leaves.

The batch should support:

- recognizing the strong-CP problem as the QCD-topology motivation for the branch,
- using Peccei-Quinn symmetry as the mechanism-side organizing concept,
- and placing axion language as the pseudo-Goldstone payoff of that symmetry-breaking
  story.

## In Scope

Likely high-value topics include:

- strong-CP problem
- Peccei-Quinn symmetry
- pseudo-Goldstone boson
- axion
- reuse of the existing theta-vacuum and spontaneous-symmetry-breaking anchors

## Explicitly Out of Scope

- technicolor and alternative electroweak-breaking model catalogs
- axion cosmology, dark-matter phenomenology, or experimental searches
- generic supersymmetry or grand-unification model building
- hadron spectroscopy or a second nonperturbative-QCD umbrella
- anomaly-calculation detail beyond the minimum background needed to orient the branch

## Expected Batch Size

- approximately 8 to 10 nodes
- approximately 7 to 10 dependency edges
- a compact partonomy linking the QCD motivation side to the symmetry-breaking payoff side

## Overlap Expectations

This batch should explicitly reuse:

- `qcd.nonperturbative_qcd_and_topology`
- `qcd.theta_vacuum`
- `sm.symmetry_breaking_and_standard_model`
- `sm.ssb.spontaneous_symmetry_breaking`
- `sm.ssb.goldstone_boson`

The batch-specific nodes here should close the remaining axion / strong-CP residue without
reopening technicolor, GUT, or cosmology branches.

## Review Risks

- letting the batch expand into a generic BSM umbrella
- detaching the strong-CP problem from the existing theta-vacuum branch
- introducing axion language without the PQ-symmetry and pseudo-Goldstone structure
- pulling technicolor, seesaw, or baryogenesis material into a branch where it does not fit

## Deliverable for This Batch

A compact authored Stage 21 neighborhood that connects theta-vacuum motivation to strong
CP, Peccei-Quinn symmetry, the pseudo-Goldstone bridge, and the axion payoff.
